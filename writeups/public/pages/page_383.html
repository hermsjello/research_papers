How i hacked 23.900.000 tumblr domains at once :)

About the Issue:

I found IDOR issue on tumblr who permit any user create arbitrary posts under any domain or inject any content. The issue use embed feature of tumblr to inject content in others tumblr accounts domains.

I could compromise 2 big tumblr servers with all domains and ‘hijack’ the domains, inject the post content, and use domains as my own.

The “embed” function at tumblr was not validating the owner of the account with the post content, this permit any user create a post and serve that arbitrary content into any domain:
